Mercenaries

Your hireling is a second character — pick the right one, gear them well, and they'll carry you.

Mercenaries follow you into battle, provide passive auras, and deal serious damage when equipped correctly. Hire them from the appropriate act NPC (Kashya, Greiz, Asheara, Qual-Kehk). The type of merc is locked in — you can only change gear and skill flavor, not switch a Rogue into a Barbarian later. Hire in Nightmare difficulty for the best skill spread.

Act 2 — Desert Mercenary (Might)

S-TIER

Aura: Might (Nightmare Offensive) — flat +% physical damage to you and party.

Why pick him

  • Best merc in the game for 90% of builds.
  • Might aura scales your damage massively — meta for melee, bowazons, javazons, summon necros.
  • Wields polearms/spears → high base damage with Insight or Infinity.

Best gear

  • Weapon: Insight (Ral-Tir-Tal-Sol, Meditation aura for infinite mana). Endgame: Infinity (Ber-Mal-Ber-Ist, Conviction aura breaks lightning immunes).
  • Armor: Fortitude (El-Sol-Dol-Lo, +300% ED + 200% enhanced defense). Budget: Treachery (Shael-Thul-Lem, IAS + fade).
  • Helm: Andariel's Visage (life leech + IAS + fire res). Budget: Vamp Gaze, Tal Rasha's Mask.

Act 2 — Desert Mercenary (Holy Freeze)

S-TIER

Aura: Holy Freeze (Nightmare Defensive) — chills all nearby enemies, slowing attacks and movement.

Why pick him

  • Best defensive merc — enemies attack ~50% slower.
  • Ideal for Hardcore, Hammerdins, Bowazons, glass-cannon casters.
  • Cannot chill cold-immune enemies (Andariel, Duriel, some Baal minions).

Best gear

  • Same as Might merc — Insight / Infinity / Fortitude / Andariel's Visage.

Act 1 — Rogue Scout (Cold/Fire)

A-TIER

Skill: Ice Arrow (Cold) or Fire Arrow / Exploding Arrow (Fire) — no aura, just ranged damage.

Why pick her

  • The only ranged merc — stays back and pelts enemies from safety.
  • Perfect for melee builds who want the merc alive in dangerous fights.
  • Cold Rogue slows enemies (soft-CC), Fire Rogue does higher raw damage.

Best gear

  • Bow: Faith (Ohm-Jah-Lem-Eld, Fanaticism aura — insane damage + attack rate). Ice is a great budget alternative.
  • Armor: Fortitude, Treachery, or Duress.
  • Helm: Andariel's Visage, Kira's Guardian (all res + CBI).

Act 5 — Barbarian (Dual-wield)

A-TIER

Skills: Bash + Stun — no aura, but dual-wields any 1H weapons and has huge HP.

Why pick him

  • Highest HP pool of any merc — extremely tanky.
  • Dual-wield → double the runeword slots (two weapons of your choice).
  • Popular for Grief dual-wielding, or a Last Wish + Grief combo for tanky builds.

Best gear

  • Weapons: Grief ×2 (Eth-Tir-Lo-Mal-Ral, massive +damage), or Last Wish + Grief, or Oath ×2 (budget).
  • Armor: Fortitude or Chains of Honor (Dol-Um-Ber-Ist, all res + magic damage reduction).
  • Helm: Arreat's Face (Barbarian-only, +2 combat skills, life leech).

Act 2 — Desert Mercenary (Prayer / Defiance / Thorns / Blessed Aim)

B-TIER

Various auras: Prayer (heal), Defiance (+DEF), Thorns (return dmg), Blessed Aim (+AR).

Why pick him

  • Prayer (Normal Combat): passive party heal — decent for Hardcore mana-starved builds.
  • Defiance (Nightmare Combat): mediocre defense boost.
  • Thorns (Normal Offensive): niche fun build, generally weak.
  • Blessed Aim (Hell Offensive): +AR aura, only useful for low-AR melee builds.

Gear

  • Same as Might merc — Insight / Fortitude / Andariel's Visage.

Act 3 — Iron Wolf (Cold/Fire/Lightning)

D-TIER

Skill: casts Glacial Spike, Fire Ball, or Charged Bolt.

Why (usually) skip him

  • Weakest merc — low damage scaling, dies quickly.
  • Uses swords + shields — very limited runeword options.
  • Only viable use: Cold Iron Wolf with Spirit sword for cheap chilling in early Hell.
TL;DR — Hire an Act 2 Nightmare Might merc the moment you can. Give him Insight polearm ASAP (Ral-Tir-Tal-Sol, one of the cheapest game-changing runewords). Upgrade to Infinity + Fortitude + Andariel's Visage for endgame. Keep a Guardian Angel or Shaftstop as backup armor while farming his gear.

Bosses & Farming

Strategies for every act boss, plus the best endgame farming spots.

Each boss has specific immunities and mechanics. Knowing them before you engage means the difference between a smooth kill and a corpse run. In Hell difficulty, most bosses gain elemental immunities — plan your damage type accordingly.

⚔️ Act Bosses

The main storyline bosses — you'll fight each one in every difficulty.

🕷️

Andariel — Act 1

Catacombs Lv 4 Immune: Fire (Hell)
  • Stack Poison Resist — her Poison Nova is the #1 killer.
  • Attack from range if possible; melee her from the sides to avoid the puke.
  • Hell: no longer fire-immune with new patches, but check immunities on your version.
  • Loot tip: After completing her quest once, re-run "Andy runs" for elite drops from a level-75+ TC.
🦂

Duriel — Act 2

Tal Rasha's Chamber Immune: Cold
  • Hardest early boss — Holy Freeze aura chills you, he hits like a truck.
  • Buy full-rejuv potions before the fight; open the TP before touching the orifice.
  • Merc will likely die instantly — resurrect and re-summon in TP if needed.
  • Ranged / caster builds have a much easier time than melee.
💀

Mephisto — Act 3

Durance of Hate Lv 3 Immune: Lightning (Hell)
  • Easiest boss in the game — famous "moat trick": stand across the blood moat, he can't cross it.
  • Use Fire/Cold/Physical damage in Hell (skip lightning sorc gear here).
  • Best MF target — level 87+ area, drops from a great TC. Meph runs are legendary.
  • Bring +Magic Find gear; kill Council members on the way for guaranteed set drops.
🔥

Diablo — Act 4

Chaos Sanctuary No immunities
  • Cap Fire and Lightning res at 75% — his Fire Hose and Lightning Hose will one-shot you otherwise.
  • Break the 3 seals slowly, rest between mini-bosses (De Seis is nasty — cold-immune melee).
  • Kite Diablo in circles; his Red Lightning Hose has a wind-up tell.
  • Chaos Sanctuary is the best XP zone from level 75+ — Chaos runs = fast leveling to 90+.
👑

Baal — Act 5

Throne of Destruction No immunities
  • Survive 5 waves in the Throne first — waves 2 (Achmel), 4 (Council), 5 (Lister the Tormentor) are hardest.
  • Baal himself: clone illusion (deals no damage), Cold Missiles, Mana Rift (drains your mana). Face-tank if you have res + life leech.
  • Best XP source at high level — "Baal runs" carry you from 90 → 99.
  • Guaranteed rune drop from Nihlathak & Baal Q1 in Hell — good rune farming.
🧊

Nihlathak — Act 5

Halls of Vaught Immune: Poison (Hell)
  • Corpse Explosion is deadly — never let dead bodies pile up around you.
  • Kill quickly with ranged/casters; melee is dangerous due to his teleport + CE combo.
  • Rewards the Halls of Anguish key for the Pandemonium Event.
  • Hell: guaranteed high-rune drop from his TC — worth farming.
🗻

The Ancients — Act 5

Arreat Summit Required quest
  • Three Barbarians — Talic (spinner), Madawc (thrower), Korlic (leaper). Fight all 3 at once.
  • They're re-rolled each attempt — quit + rejoin the game if the affix combo is unwinnable (e.g. all three Fire Enchanted + Extra Strong in Hell).
  • Bring plenty of full rejuv potions. Merc dies fast here.
  • Kill Madawc (thrower) first — his hits from range are brutal.
🌌

Ubers — Pandemonium Event

Endgame 3 keys required
  • Farm 3 keys in Hell: Key of Terror (Countess), Key of Hate (Nihlathak / Summoner), Key of Destruction (Nihlathak) — wait, correct: Terror (Countess), Hate (Summoner in Arcane Sanctuary), Destruction (Nihlathak).
  • Cube 1 of each key → opens portal to one of 3 uber mini-bosses (Uber Izual, Uber Duriel, Uber Andariel).
  • Cube their 3 body parts → portal to Uber Tristram (Uber Mephisto + Uber Diablo + Uber Baal, all together).
  • Reward: Hellfire Torch — grand charm with +3 to a class's skills, +20 stats, +res.
  • Melee builds thrive here (Smiter Paladin is meta). Pure casters struggle due to physical damage reduction.

🛡️ Breaking Hell Immunities

In Hell, 30-50% of monsters are immune to a given element. Here's how to punch through.

Immunity math: A monster is "immune" when its resistance is ≥100%. You need to reduce it below 100% to deal damage — but reductions past that point are only 1/5 as effective. So -100% enemy res only actually removes 20% from an immune enemy.

Conviction aura (Infinity): Best immunity-breaker in the game. At level 12 (from Infinity runeword), reduces lightning/fire/cold res by ~85%, breaking most Hell lightning immunes.
Lower Resist curse (Necromancer): Second-best. Wands with +3 Lower Resist plus a Necro's own points can hit -70% or more.
-% enemy res on gear: Some uniques/runewords (Death's Fathom, Crescent Moon, etc.) have "-X% to enemy X resistance" — stack these for casters.
Cold Mastery (Sorc only): Passive -res to cold only, works on immunes at 1/5 efficiency.

Breakpoints

Diablo II uses discrete animation frames — extra % between breakpoints does nothing.

The game runs at 25 frames per second. Attack speed, cast speed, hit-recovery, and block are measured in frames. Reaching the next breakpoint shaves off one frame — anything in between is wasted stat budget. Below are the meta breakpoints per class. Bold entries are the practical endgame targets.

Survival Tips

Essential knowledge for new and returning adventurers.

🎲

Horadric Cube Recipes

Combine 3 Perfect Gems of the same type to upgrade them. Transmute 3 chipped gems + a magic sword for a socketed rare sword. The Cube is your best crafting tool.

💎

Socketing Strategy

Use Shael rune in weapons for +20% IAS. Ort rune adds lightning damage. Lum and Ko runes in armor boost energy and dexterity. Plan sockets before spending runes.

🗺️

Magic Find Farming

Mephisto and Pindleskin are top MF targets. Stack Magic Find gear to increase rare and unique drop rates. 200–400% MF is a sweet spot without diminishing returns.

⚡

Resistances Matter

Cap all resistances at 75% in Hell difficulty. Prioritize fire and cold resistance early. The Paladin's salvation aura or a rare amulet with +30% all res can save your life.

🧪

Potion Management

Keep your belt full at all times. Use the hotkeys (1–4) for quick drinking. Healing + mana potions alternated on your belt rows means you can chug them faster in emergencies.

🤝

Mercenary Builds

Act 2 (Nightmare) defensive mercenary with Infinity polearm is the most popular. Give your merc good armor and a Life Steal helm. They provide auras that massively boost your damage.

🔄

Difficulty Progression

Complete Normal → Nightmare → Hell. Each difficulty amplifies monster HP and damage. Don't rush — complete quests for stat/skill point rewards in each difficulty.

🏃

Faster Cast Rate Breakpoints

FCR works in breakpoints: 63%, 105%, 200% for Sorceress teleport. Hitting the right FCR breakpoint is more valuable than raw +% since in-between values do nothing.

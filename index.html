Mercenaries

Your hireling is a second character — pick the right one, gear them well, and they'll carry you.

Mercenaries follow you into battle, provide passive auras, and deal serious damage when equipped correctly. Hire them from the appropriate act NPC (Kashya, Greiz, Asheara, Qual-Kehk). The type of merc is locked in — you can only change gear and skill flavor, not switch a Rogue into a Barbarian later. Hire in Nightmare difficulty for the best skill spread.

Act 2 — Desert Mercenary (Might)

S-TIER

Aura: Might (Nightmare Offensive) — flat +% physical damage to you and party.

Why pick him

  • Best merc in the game for 90% of builds.
  • Might aura scales your damage massively — meta for melee, bowazons, javazons, summon necros.
  • Wields polearms/spears → high base damage with Insight or Infinity.

Best gear

  • Weapon: Insight (Ral-Tir-Tal-Sol, Meditation aura for infinite mana). Endgame: Infinity (Ber-Mal-Ber-Ist, Conviction aura breaks lightning immunes).
  • Armor: Fortitude (El-Sol-Dol-Lo, +300% ED + 200% enhanced defense). Budget: Treachery (Shael-Thul-Lem, IAS + fade).
  • Helm: Andariel's Visage (life leech + IAS + fire res). Budget: Vamp Gaze, Tal Rasha's Mask.

Act 2 — Desert Mercenary (Holy Freeze)

S-TIER

Aura: Holy Freeze (Nightmare Defensive) — chills all nearby enemies, slowing attacks and movement.

Why pick him

  • Best defensive merc — enemies attack ~50% slower.
  • Ideal for Hardcore, Hammerdins, Bowazons, glass-cannon casters.
  • Cannot chill cold-immune enemies (Andariel, Duriel, some Baal minions).

Best gear

  • Same as Might merc — Insight / Infinity / Fortitude / Andariel's Visage.

Act 1 — Rogue Scout (Cold/Fire)

A-TIER

Skill: Ice Arrow (Cold) or Fire Arrow / Exploding Arrow (Fire) — no aura, just ranged damage.

Why pick her

  • The only ranged merc — stays back and pelts enemies from safety.
  • Perfect for melee builds who want the merc alive in dangerous fights.
  • Cold Rogue slows enemies (soft-CC), Fire Rogue does higher raw damage.

Best gear

  • Bow: Faith (Ohm-Jah-Lem-Eld, Fanaticism aura — insane damage + attack rate). Ice is a great budget alternative.
  • Armor: Fortitude, Treachery, or Duress.
  • Helm: Andariel's Visage, Kira's Guardian (all res + CBI).

Act 5 — Barbarian (Dual-wield)

A-TIER

Skills: Bash + Stun — no aura, but dual-wields any 1H weapons and has huge HP.

Why pick him

  • Highest HP pool of any merc — extremely tanky.
  • Dual-wield → double the runeword slots (two weapons of your choice).
  • Popular for Grief dual-wielding, or a Last Wish + Grief combo for tanky builds.

Best gear

  • Weapons: Grief ×2 (Eth-Tir-Lo-Mal-Ral, massive +damage), or Last Wish + Grief, or Oath ×2 (budget).
  • Armor: Fortitude or Chains of Honor (Dol-Um-Ber-Ist, all res + magic damage reduction).
  • Helm: Arreat's Face (Barbarian-only, +2 combat skills, life leech).

Act 2 — Desert Mercenary (Prayer / Defiance / Thorns / Blessed Aim)

B-TIER

Various auras: Prayer (heal), Defiance (+DEF), Thorns (return dmg), Blessed Aim (+AR).

Why pick him

  • Prayer (Normal Combat): passive party heal — decent for Hardcore mana-starved builds.
  • Defiance (Nightmare Combat): mediocre defense boost.
  • Thorns (Normal Offensive): niche fun build, generally weak.
  • Blessed Aim (Hell Offensive): +AR aura, only useful for low-AR melee builds.

Gear

  • Same as Might merc — Insight / Fortitude / Andariel's Visage.

🛡️ Breaking Hell Immunities

In Hell, 30-50% of monsters are immune to a given element. Here's how to punch through.

Immunity math: A monster is "immune" when its resistance is ≥100%. You need to reduce it below 100% to deal damage — but reductions past that point are only 1/5 as effective. So -100% enemy res only actually removes 20% from an immune enemy.

Conviction aura (Infinity): Best immunity-breaker in the game. At level 12 (from Infinity runeword), reduces lightning/fire/cold res by ~85%, breaking most Hell lightning immunes.
Lower Resist curse (Necromancer): Second-best. Wands with +3 Lower Resist plus a Necro's own points can hit -70% or more.
-% enemy res on gear: Some uniques/runewords (Death's Fathom, Crescent Moon, etc.) have "-X% to enemy X resistance" — stack these for casters.
Cold Mastery (Sorc only): Passive -res to cold only, works on immunes at 1/5 efficiency.

📊 Treasure Class (TC) Reference

Pro Tips & Deep Mechanics

The game doesn't teach you any of this. These are the things that separate rushers from veterans.

💤

Save & Exit = Full Heal

Beginner

The best "potion" in the game costs nothing.

  • Save & Exit → rejoin your game refills HP, mana, and merc HP instantly.
  • Also resets act boss quests aren't affected (you keep your progress).
  • Use before every hard fight — Duriel, Ancients, Ubers, hardcore emergencies.
  • Downside: monsters respawn outside towns, so re-clear paths as needed.
🎯

Free Merc Healing

Beginner

Stop paying to revive — just talk.

  • Chat with Akara, Charsi, Ormus, or Malah to fully heal your merc for free.
  • Revive cost scales with merc's level — a level-90 merc costs 40,000+ gold each death.
  • Keep them healed after every difficult pack, especially before quest bosses.
🎒

Inventory Hotkeys

Beginner

The controls the tutorial never taught you.

  • Shift+click a stack in a vendor = buy the whole stack.
  • Ctrl+click item = send to stash or shared stash instantly.
  • Alt held down = highlights all items on ground (essential in loot piles).
  • Right-click the Horadric Cube from inventory = opens it (no need to place on ground).
  • W = swap weapons instantly (equip a CtA + shield combo on secondary for BO).
⚡

Static Field Rule of Thirds

Beginner

Static doesn't kill in Hell — it softens.

  • Static Field reduces enemy HP by 25% per cast — but caps out at 1/3 HP in Hell difficulty.
  • 2-3 casts is enough; more is wasted mana. Follow up with your kill spell.
  • Doesn't work on Uber bosses, Baal clone, or Act bosses in some patches.
🎁

Save Your Quest Rewards

Beginner

Some quests have one-shot rewards worth saving.

  • Andariel quest drop — biased roll, save for after your first level-up in Hell.
  • Charsi's Imbue — one rare item per difficulty. Save for a high-item-level Circlet or gloves in Hell.
  • Radament's book = free skill point per difficulty. Do it.
  • Anya's scroll of resistance = +10 max res per difficulty (up to +30 all!). Never skip.
  • Ancients quest = 2 free skill points per difficulty in Hell (huge).
🔥

Breaking Hell Immunities

Combat

Immunity ≠ invincible — you just need enough negative resistance.

  • Immune = ≥ 100% resistance. Reduce below 100% to deal damage.
  • Below 100%, further -res is only 1/5 as effective (100 -res = only 20 res removed).
  • Best breakers: Infinity (Conviction -85%), Lower Resist curse, gear with "-X% enemy res".
  • Cold Sorc's Cold Mastery only works on the cold immune at 1/5 efficiency (still worth it).
Math: Enemy at 110% cold res + -85% Conviction = 25% (not immune) → your damage applies normally.
🛡️

Cap Resistances at 75%

Combat

Hell difficulty applies a resistance penalty you can't ignore.

  • Hell penalty: -100 to all resists (Normal 0, Nightmare -40, Hell -100).
  • Max resistance is 75% by default (higher only with +max res mods like Guardian Angel, Anya's scroll).
  • Priority order: Lightning > Fire > Cold > Poison (lightning uniques hit hardest).
  • Any resist below 75% = you're taking full damage on that element.
💥

Physical Damage Reduction

Combat

PDR (%) and DR (flat) stack multiplicatively.

  • Flat DR (e.g. Verdungo's "Damage Reduced by 15") applies first — subtracted from every physical hit.
  • %DR (e.g. Shaftstop "30% DR") applies second on the remaining damage.
  • Hard cap: 50% physical damage reduction from %DR (uber bosses have 50% innate!).
  • Combine both types for the toughest builds (Barb, Smiter, Necromancer bone armor stack).
🩸

Life Leech Rules

Combat

Not all enemies bleed — plan accordingly.

  • Life leech = % of physical damage dealt returned as HP.
  • Undead (skeletons, zombies, mummies): 50% reduced leech in Nightmare, none in Hell.
  • Constructs (mechanical, elementals): 0% leech always — you cannot leech from them.
  • Chaos Sanctuary Doom Knights? Undead — bring pots. Iron Golems in Uber Trist? No leech.
  • Melee builds should stack 10-15% leech + healing on hit ("Replenish Life" mod).
🎯

Amplify Damage = King

Combat

The single biggest damage multiplier in the game.

  • Amp Damage curse: -100% physical resist, effectively 2× physical damage.
  • Sources: Necro curse, Amn rune (5% CtC on striking), Atma's Scarab amulet, Cat's Eye.
  • Every physical-damage build should carry an Amp Damage source. Every one.
  • Decrepify is stronger (50% PDR + slow) but shorter duration. Amp lasts longer.
❄️

Cannot Be Frozen is Mandatory

Combat

Getting chilled in Hell = dead.

  • Cold damage from Gloams, Nihlathak, Baal's minions can freeze you for 3+ seconds.
  • Frozen = can't attack, can't cast, can't drink potions.
  • Sources of Cannot Be Frozen: Raven Frost ring, Dwarf Star ring, Rite of Passage quest reward (Hell only!).
  • Non-negotiable for melee builds and softcore Ubers, mandatory for hardcore.
👥

/players 8 — Free Loot Multiplier

Loot

One command; massively better drops.

  • Type /players 8 in single-player or offline to simulate an 8-player game.
  • Drop rate boost: ~7× vs. /players 1 for uniques and sets.
  • XP boost: ~4-5× more XP per kill.
  • Cost: monsters have 4× HP and hit slightly harder. Nothing you can't handle.
  • Always run /players 8 (or 7, or 5) when farming. There is no reason not to.
✨

MF Diminishing Returns

Loot

More MF isn't always better past a certain point.

  • MF applies with diminishing returns using this formula: effectiveMF = MF × 250 / (MF + 250) for uniques.
  • Practical: 200-300% MF is the sweet spot. 400% is diminishing. 800% is nearly wasted.
  • Effective unique MF at 300% = 136%. At 800% = 190%. Massive stat sacrifice for little gain.
  • Kill speed > MF. A build with 200% MF that kills 3× faster beats a 500% MF build.
Rule of thumb: 200-300% MF on farmers, 0% MF on bossers/rushers.
💰

Gambling for Uniques

Loot

The lottery for rings, amulets, and circlets.

  • Vendor gamble odds: 10% rare, ~1 in 2000 unique per attempt.
  • Best gamble targets: Circlets/Coronets (Griffon's chance!), Amulets (Mara's/Highlord's/SoJ), Rings (SoJ/Raven Frost/Bul-Kathos).
  • Your character level must be ≥ item required level for unique to be possible.
  • Coronets gambled at lvl 92+ can roll Griffon's Eye. Best gold sink in the game.
  • Amulets/Rings/Circlets keep same rarity odds across categories — pick the highest-value ones.
💎

Perfect Gem Recipes

Loot

The Cube's most-used recipes.

  • 3 perfect gems (same type) + magic item = reroll magic to new random magic.
  • 3 flawless gems (same type) = 1 perfect gem.
  • 3 identical uniques → cube shuffles into a different unique of the same type (risky!).
  • Ral + Sol + perfect emerald + magic weapon = magic weapon of same type with sockets rolled.
  • 3 chipped gems + magic sword = same sword socketed. Great early-game runeword base.
🏪

Vendor Item Levels Are Fixed

Loot

Where you shop determines what you can roll.

  • Vendor items have an ilvl = the difficulty's fixed area level for that vendor.
  • For 4-socket 3os armors or Superior bases, shop in Hell Act 5 vendors (Anya, Malah).
  • For Cube recipes needing high-ilvl bases, buy from later-act vendors.
  • Fara (Act 2) sells Long War Bows; Anya sells Colossus Voulges. Know your vendor.

FHR Breakpoints

Advanced

Faster Hit Recovery = stop-locking in a stunlock kills you.

  • Getting hit interrupts your action — FHR reduces that interrupt animation.
  • Sorc FHR: 9% → 6 frames, 24% → 5, 48% → 4, 86% → 3 (cap for most).
  • Barb FHR: 9% → 6, 20% → 5, 42% → 4, 86% → 3.
  • Melee builds MUST hit 86% FHR minimum. Casters aim for 48% or 86%.
  • Sources: Shael rune, Stealth armor, Twitchthroe, most rare belts/gloves.
⚔️

IAS Weapon Speed Modifier

Advanced

IAS isn't just IAS — the weapon's speed matters more.

  • Effective attack rate = weapon base speed + IAS + skill IAS, capped by breakpoints.
  • A "-30 speed" weapon (Berserker Axe) hits faster than a "+0 speed" weapon (Colossus Blade) at same IAS.
  • Grief in Phase Blade (-30 speed) is meta for melee — hits max breakpoint with minimal IAS.
  • Zeal / Fury / Whirlwind each have their own breakpoints — different from normal attack.
🎲

Deadly Strike + Critical Strike

Advanced

They stack additively for a huge combined chance.

  • Both effects double physical damage on trigger (not quadruple when both roll).
  • They roll independently — DS is checked first; if it fails, CS is checked.
  • Effective 2× chance = 1 - (1-DS) × (1-CS).
  • Example: 50% DS + 50% CS = 75% chance of a double-damage hit.
  • Gore Rider (15% DS) + Highlord's (up to 34% DS by clvl) + Passion runeword = frequent doubles.
🌀

Chance to Cast (CtC) Layering

Advanced

Passive spells that fire off your attacks — free damage.

  • Every "X% chance to cast Y" mod rolls independently per hit or per struck.
  • Best synergies: Dracul's Grasp (Life Tap on strike) + Guillaume's Face (35% CS) + physical build.
  • Necromancer Bone Prison from gear = free pack lockdown.
  • Watch out for "chance to cast on death" — can kill your own merc via CE.
  • Casters proccing melee spells (Nova, Chain Lightning) = extra AoE without spending points.
🏛️

Enigma is the Meta Endgame

Advanced

Teleport for every class = game-changing.

  • Enigma runeword grants +1 Teleport — every non-sorc can teleport.
  • Also: +2 all skills, +1 str per level, +14 life per level, huge MF, damage reduce.
  • Runes: Jah + Ith + Ber — one of the most expensive runewords.
  • Best base: Mage Plate (light) or Archon Plate (Hell-only, endgame).
  • The single biggest QoL upgrade in the game — every farmer needs one eventually.
🎓

Prevent Monster Heal

Advanced

Some bosses regen HP — this mod stops it dead.

  • "Prevent Monster Heal" mod = boss cannot regenerate HP mid-fight.
  • Critical for slow-kill fights: Ubers, high-players Baal, hardcore Duriel.
  • Sources: Rune Ort in weapon (rare), Nature's Peace ring, Death runeword.
  • Without it, a Uber Mephisto slowly regens back to full while you're dodging.
🎯

Attack Rating & Chance to Hit

Advanced

Missing attacks in Hell = the silent killer.

  • Chance to Hit = 2 × AR / (AR + Defense) × (yourLvl / (yourLvl + monsterLvl)), capped 5-95%.
  • Hell monster defense is huge — a bare-bones AR of 2000 might have only 50% CtH.
  • Solutions: Angelic Halo + Amulet set (crazy AR), Blessed Aim merc, Passion runeword (+150 AR).
  • Uncanny AR sources: Frostburn (+40% AR), Highlord's (+level% AR).
  • Skills that ignore AR: Berserk, Blessed Hammer, Smite, Frozen Orb, Fist of the Heavens. These never miss.

Public Game Etiquette

Multiplayer

Get invited back to Baal runs.

  • Baal runs: everyone kills waves together, host kills Baal. Don't grief.
  • Chaos runs: break seals in order — Vizier, De Seis, Infector. Wait for team on De Seis (cold-immune melee is deadly).
  • Loot etiquette: Don't ninja rare drops in front of the host. Ask first.
  • "pubby" (public game): assume nothing; announce your intent ("Meph run? mind if I clear Council?").
📈

Party XP Bonuses

Multiplayer

The reason to run in a group.

  • XP is divided among party members within a screen — but with a +35% party XP bonus.
  • Also: monsters are stronger with more players, which multiplies XP too.
  • Best XP: full 8-man Baal runs (level 90+ range) = 5-10× solo speed.
  • Stay within party range (near the screen edge) or you get NO XP from kills.
🚫

Hostile & Player-vs-Player

Multiplayer

The button that ruins friendships.

  • Right-click a player portrait → Hostile = flag for PvP. Takes 5 seconds to activate.
  • Hostile players deal 17% of listed damage to each other (huge nerf vs. PvE numbers).
  • Elemental damage in PvP has additional 1/6 penalty (except lightning).
  • Cannot Be Frozen ring is mandatory for PvP too — freeze-locking is the top-tier strategy.
  • Never hostile in someone's game without asking — it's game-over etiquette.
💱

Rune & Item Trade Values

Multiplayer

Know what your loot is worth before you sell.

  • Currency = high runes. Vex/Ohm/Lo/Sur/Ber/Jah/Cham/Zod in ascending order.
  • Ber ≈ standard bulk trade unit ("1 Ber"). Everything else is measured in Ber-equivalents.
  • Perfect Skulls, Perfect Amethysts, and Ist runes = mid-tier currency for cheaper items.
  • Never accept a trade window without reading every item — scam artists swap items last-second.
  • Trust nobody's "muling for a friend" — always trade item-for-item, no gold-only deals for HRs.
🎪

Rush Etiquette

Multiplayer

Getting rushed to Hell — a rite of passage.

  • A "rush" = high-level player kills quest bosses so a new character can enter next difficulty.
  • You must be alive and in the game when the boss dies to get the quest completion.
  • Duriel + Diablo + Baal quests are the essential ones per difficulty.
  • Tips for the rushed player: keep a Town Portal book handy, don't wander, don't die.
  • Payment: usually a Pul rune, Ist rune, or a specific item the rusher wants.
